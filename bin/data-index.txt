* keyserver is always in an enclave     (rsa_priv_enc() on each request)
* cache is always 16k (smallest possible)
* file is always 1k.txt
* 1 nginx worker
* enclaves are always exitless
* nextfs filesystem images are always size=128M, blocksize=1024, and type=ext2.
* bdcrypt always uses aes-256-xts

So, smdish and smuf are like bdvericrypt
smc is like bdcrypt
what you are missing is a smverity (svm)  (a la bdverity).
